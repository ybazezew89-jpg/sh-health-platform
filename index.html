What is Hypertension?

High blood pressure means your blood pushes too hard against your blood vessels. If untreated, it can lead to heart disease and stroke.

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes occurs when your blood sugar is too high. Healthy eating, exercise, and regular monitoring help control it.

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily
  • Reduce salt and sugar intake
  • Drink enough clean water
