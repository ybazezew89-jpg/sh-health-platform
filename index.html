What is Hypertension?

High blood pressure means your blood pushes too hard against your blood vessels. If untreated, it can lead to heart disease and stroke.

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes occurs when your blood sugar is too high. Healthy eating, regular exercise, and monitoring help control it.

Lung Health & Breathing

Conditions like Asthma and COPD make it harder for air to move through your lungs. Keep your home free of dust and smoke, and always keep your prescribed inhaler nearby.

Tip: Practice deep breathing exercises to help strengthen your lung capacity.

Kidney Care

Your kidneys filter waste from your blood. High blood pressure and diabetes are the leading causes of kidney damage. Drinking water and reducing salt intake are the best ways to protect them.

Simple Talk: Think of your kidneys as your body's natural cleaning system.

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily
  • Reduce salt and sugar intake
  • Drink enough clean water
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol
